About

ARES Gridiron Market is the American football market board.

It ranks pro and college players while tracking the broader development chain.

It is not an official league product. It is an independent analytical database.

High school, JUCO, FCS, UFL, CFL, and transfer portal players are tracked separately until data quality supports fair ranking against pro or college boards.
